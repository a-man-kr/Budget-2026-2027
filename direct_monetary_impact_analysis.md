#### Critical Impact Quantification

**NSE F&O Market Size:**

- Daily Futures Turnover: ~‚Çπ15,000-20,000 Cr
- Annual STT Collection (FY26E): ~‚Çπ35,000 Cr

**Per-Trade Impact:**
| Trade Type | Old STT | New STT | Retail Trader Impact |
|:-----------|:--------|:--------|:---------------------|

**Broker Revenue Impact:**
| Company | F&O Revenue % | Estimated Impact |
|:--------|:--------------|:-----------------|

- ‚ùå Market volatility could offset with urgent hedging needs
- ‚ùå Premium pricing segment (Angel One Pro) may grow

#### Investment Action
